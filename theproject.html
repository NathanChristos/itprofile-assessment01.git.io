Nathan - Swiss Army Knife - Christos resides in beautiful sunny Queensland where he tirelessly tackles the world’s electronic security and surveillance issues. He is never afraid to get his hands dirty and always hungry to learn something not yet known. Born in Australia with European heritage, Nathan spent his early years far from the IT world, growing up amongst the farming communities in mid NSW. Home schooled from an early age, he was noted at being years above the requirement of his curriculum but sadly, didn’t continue his vocational journey through to university until recently when he started studying a Bachelor of Information Technology at RMIT. Nathan has enjoyed mastering many skills throughout his life as he has always had a strong desire to learn anything unfamiliar. Growing up with a musical family, Nathan inevitably became a proficient musician and performer, spending many years professionally playing in bands. As a physical outlet, Nathan has been practicing and teaching Kung Fu since early 2009 and has recently headed up his own branch school in the northern Gold Coast region. Nathan also enjoys pursuing a pseudo career in photography and videography that he found a knack for through his involvement in on-screen acting.
